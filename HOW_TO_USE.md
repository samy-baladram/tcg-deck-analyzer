# 🚀 How to Use the App (No Code Interface Needed)

## Opening the App - Super Simple!

### From Codespaces:
1. **Open Codespaces** → Go to your repository and click "Code" → "Codespaces" → Open your space
2. **Wait ~30 seconds** for the environment to load
3. **The app will automatically launch** - you'll see a notification at the bottom right
4. **Click the browser icon or URL** in the notification to open the app
5. **That's it!** The app is now running in your browser

**The app will stay running as long as your Codespaces is open** - just refresh the browser tab if needed.

---

## What's Happening Behind the Scenes

- ✅ Docker container auto-starts
- ✅ Python environment auto-activates  
- ✅ App auto-runs on `localhost:8501`
- ✅ Browser auto-opens to the app
- ✅ Streamlit UI is in "minimal" mode (cleaner interface)

---

## If You Ever Need to Restart

- Click the ↻ (refresh) icon in your browser, OR
- Click the Streamlit menu (☰) at the top right → "Rerun"

---

## Accessing from Your Computer

If you're on your machine and Codespaces is running in the cloud:
- The Codespaces interface **automatically forwards** the port to your browser
- Just use the URL that appears - it's publicly accessible as long as Codespaces is running
- Close Codespaces to stop the app

---

## That's All!

No command line. No terminal. Just open Codespaces and use the app like any other website.
